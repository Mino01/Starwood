Structural Transformer: Musical Coherence in Latent Space

The Structural Transformer operates directly in RAVE's continuous latent space, generating a single, dense vector that encapsulates the entire musical piece's structure. This approach is highly efficient for modeling long-form music while maintaining semantic coherence.

By conditioning on the Unified Control Embedding, the Transformer learns to generate musically coherent latent representations that capture melody, harmony, rhythm, and overall structure.
