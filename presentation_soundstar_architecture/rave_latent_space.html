RAVE: Real-time Latent Space for High-Fidelity Texture

RAVE (Realtime Audio Variational autoEncoder) provides the primary musical language for Soundstar's Transformer. Its robust, continuous latent space enables efficient modeling of long-form music while maintaining high-fidelity audio reconstruction.

Key Capabilities
Continuous latent representation
Real-time audio decoding (20× faster than real-time)
High-fidelity stereo output at 44.1kHz
Efficient long-form music modeling
Seamless integration with Transformer
