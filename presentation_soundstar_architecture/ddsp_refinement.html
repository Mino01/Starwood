DDSP Refinement Layer: Interpretable Timbre Control

The DDSP Refinement Net acts as a critical translator, converting the abstract RAVE latent vector into interpretable, physical control parameters. This enables fine-grained manipulation of the audio's timbre and characteristics.

By mapping the latent space to explicit DSP controls, users gain unprecedented control over the generated audio without requiring model retraining.

Key Advantage
Refine timbre and audio characteristics on-the-fly by adjusting DDSP parameters, providing a bridge between abstract generation and precise sound design.
